# UX-Savvy Front-End Developer with a Passion for React, GraphQL, Rest, Next.js, and AI

We are looking for a talented front-end developer with a passion for the latest technologies, including AI, to join our team. You will be working with one of the most creative and passionate teams, all focused on completely changing the way people interact and work.
The ideal candidate will be a creative problem solver with a strong UX focus, and a love for creating beautiful, responsive interfaces that delight users.


## Responsibilities

- Develop visually stunning and intuitive user interfaces.
- Write clean, reusable, and scalable code that follows best practices.
- Keep up-to-date with emerging trends and technologies in front-end development, AI, and UX.
- Ensure the technical feasibility of UI/UX designs and optimize applications for maximum speed and scalability.
- Work closely with our design and development teams to create engaging user experiences that meet business requirements.
- Contribute to our vision of completely changing the way people interact with customers using machines.

## Requirements

- Strong proficiency in React, GraphQL, Rest, and Next.js, with a solid understanding of software engineering principles.
- Experience with AI and machine learning technologies is a plus.
- A strong portfolio of front-end development projects that demonstrate your skills in creating engaging and responsive user interfaces.
- Passion for UX design and a deep understanding of user-centered design principles.
- Excellent problem-solving skills, attention to detail, and ability to work independently or in a team environment.
- Excellent communication skills and ability to collaborate effectively with remote teams.
- A strong desire to help us completely change the way people interact with customers using machines.

## Got what it takes?

If you are a UX-savvy front-end developer with a passion for enhancing human interaction through AI, and have the desire to work on one of the most exciting projects that is pushing the boundaries of technology and design, then this is the chance for you to join a fun, dynamic, and innovative team that values creativity, curiosity, and teamwork that will change the world. 

Send us your application [here][application] about why you are the right person to help us take on this massive undertaking, and we'll be in touch!
